# Google Ads Extensions (Assets)

Google renamed "extensions" to "assets" but the functionality is the same. Extensions expand your ad with additional information and improve CTR.

## Extension Types

### Sitelink Assets
Additional links below your ad pointing to specific pages.

- **Min 2, recommended 4-8** per campaign/ad group
- Each sitelink: headline (25 chars) + 2 description lines (35 chars each)
- Link to distinct, relevant pages (not all to homepage)
- Examples: "Free Consultation", "Pricing", "Case Studies", "Contact Us"

### Callout Assets
Short text highlights (no links). Show benefits/features.

- **Recommended 4-6** per campaign
- Max 25 characters each
- Examples: "Free Shipping", "24/7 Support", "No Contract", "Same-Day Delivery"
- Don't duplicate info from sitelinks

### Structured Snippet Assets
Predefined headers with a list of values. Showcase product/service categories.

- **Headers:** Amenities, Brands, Courses, Destinations, Featured hotels, Insurance coverage, Models, Neighborhoods, Service catalog, Shows, Styles, Types
- **Min 3 values** per header
- Example: Header "Types" → "Search Ads, Display Ads, Video Ads, PMax"

### Call Assets
Phone number displayed with your ad. Click-to-call on mobile.

- Show only during business hours (set a schedule)
- Use a trackable number for conversion attribution
- Works best for service businesses

### Location Assets
Show business address, map, distance. Linked via Google Business Profile.

- Requires linked Google Business Profile
- Best for businesses with physical locations
- Drives store visits and local awareness

### Price Assets
Show products/services with prices. Users can browse before clicking.

- **Types:** Brands, Events, Locations, Neighborhoods, Product categories, Product tiers, Service categories, Service tiers, Services
- Min 3 items, up to 8
- Each item: header (25 chars), description (25 chars), price, URL

### Image Assets
Visual images alongside your text ads.

- **Landscape:** 1200x628 (min 600x314), aspect ratio 1.91:1
- **Square:** 1200x1200 (min 300x300)
- Keep important content in center 80% (safe area for cropping)
- No logos, text overlays, or buttons in images
- At least 1 landscape + 1 square recommended

### Lead Form Assets
In-ad lead capture form (name, email, phone).

- Pre-filled with Google account data for higher completion rates
- Configurable fields and qualifying questions
- Webhook or CSV download for leads
- Best for mobile users

### Promotion Assets
Highlight sales, discounts, promo codes.

- Occasion-based (Black Friday, Back to School, etc.) or no occasion
- Show discount as percentage, monetary, or "up to"
- Set start/end dates for automatic scheduling

### Business Name & Logo
Displays your verified business name and logo.

- Requires advertiser verification
- Enhances brand recognition
- Automatically applied once verified

## Extension Strategy

1. **Always add:** Sitelinks, callouts, structured snippets (minimum viable extensions)
2. **Add if relevant:** Call (services), location (physical stores), image (visual products)
3. **Add for promotions:** Promotion assets during sales periods
4. **Add for lead gen:** Lead form assets for high-mobile-traffic campaigns
5. **Set at campaign level** as default, override at ad group level for specificity
6. **Review performance** quarterly — remove underperformers, test new variations
